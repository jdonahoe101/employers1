About Employers One Source Group

Outsourcing of employment administration is what Professional Employer Organizations (PEO's) do. Simply put, outsourcing is allowing a PEO to handle a portion of your business for you. PEO's handle the most common services associated with operating a business. Among those services are payroll administration, health insurance procurement & benefits administration, workers compensation & risk management, and human resources. The effect of all of this is to allow you the business owner to concentrate on your core business and not tie up your staff with non-productive non-revenue generating tasks.

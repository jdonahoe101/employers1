Welcome to Employers One Source Group

A Professional Employer Organization

As a PEO, our primary objective is to work hand-in-hand with our clients to allow them to focus on the services and functions that are core to their business. Your organization will not be burdened with the chores of administering payroll or filing government forms associated with having employees. We work with our clients so that they can gain the strength of our experiences, and the efficiency of our size. In effect, our clients have the benefits of a much larger business.

At EOSG our focus is to establish strong, long-lasting relationships with our clients. We build trust with our clients so they know that when they call us, they will have someone looking out for their best interests. We have earned a reputation for being precise with our processes and have streamlined procedures for checks and balances to ensure accuracy.
